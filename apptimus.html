What is Apptimus

Apptimus is a mobile app that simplifies workspace management with real-time collaboration and actionable insights. Built for dynamic enterprises, it enables seamless control anytime, anywhere

Overview

Imagine walking into a workplace where everything flows effortlessly—your desk is already booked, your visitor is welcomed without hassle, your meeting room is ready, and any service request is handled instantly. Apptimus brings this experience to life with its smart modules for Desk Booking, Visitor Management, Meeting Room Management, and Service Request Management, making workspaces more connected, efficient, and people-friendly.

These small daily hurdles add up, slowing down productivity and creating frustration across the organisation. lets see what are the challenges are faced by users

Employees waste time searching for available desks.

Picture this—you walk into the office and instantly know where your desk is, your guest checks in without any hassle, the meeting room is free right on time, and even that little service request gets fixed before it slows you down. With Apptimus, all those everyday workplace struggles turn into smooth, stress-free moments.

Research

Initial Research

While designing Apptimus, I connected with employees, managers, and facility teams to learn their everyday struggles—from desk hunts to meeting room clashes. These insights helped me craft simple, friendly solutions that make workplace life smoother and more joyful.

Defining the users:

Before designing Apptimus, we asked a simple question—who’s really using this app every day? From employees hunting for desks, to managers juggling teams, to facility staff keeping things running, and admins overseeing it all—everyone had their own story. By stepping into their shoes, we could design solutions that felt natural, helpful, and easy for each of them.

We identified three key user groups:

Employees

Roles: Staff, Associates, Team Members

Focus: Desk booking, meeting room reservations, service requests, and day-to-day workplace navigation

Managers

Roles: Team Leads, Project Managers, Department Managers

Focus: Student onboarding, fee tracking, placement data - management, and departmental coordination

Administrators

Roles: Admin Staff, System Owners

Focus: Overseeing overall operations, monitoring usage, managing configurations

Interviews:

With Apptimus, we focused on real workplace moments—booking desks, finding meeting rooms, welcoming visitors, managing lockers and passes, tracking attendance, and raising tickets or surveys. Each feature was designed to remove friction and make the workday smoother, smarter, and more enjoyable.

A few Pain points:

  • Limited visibility of co-worker bookings.
  • Difficulty finding available desks quickly.
  • Double bookings or unavailability at peak times.
  • Struggles in finding a suitable room based on size, facilities (projector, whiteboard, etc.), or location.
  • Locker booking flow feels confusing or hidden
  • Employees rarely know where or how to request lockers
  • Security teams find it hard to track multiple visitors efficiently

Wish lists:

  • Easy view of where teammates are sitting
  • Clear floor plan view with live availability
  • Visibility of available lockers across floors/buildings.
  • Digital visitor passes for faster entry.
  • Quick pre-registration for visitors with auto check-in
  • Real-time reports and usage insights

Booking a Desk with Amenities

Imagine an employee starting their day with Apptimus. Instead of walking around the office searching for a free desk, they simply open the app. They begin by choosing the date and time they’ll be in the office. With flexible options for duration or shift, they can easily match their booking to their work schedule.
Next comes personalisation. Employees can select amenities – whether it’s a dual monitor for design tasks, an ergonomic chair for comfort, or proximity to charging points. This ensures their desk is not just available, but also tailored to their needs.
Moving to the interactive floor map, they see a clear visual of available, occupied, and selected desks. A single tap secures their preferred spot. Finally, the app confirms the booking with all details neatly summarised – desk number, location, time, and chosen amenities – giving the employee confidence that everything is ready when they arrive.
With Apptimus, booking a desk isn’t just about finding a seat – it’s about creating the right workspace, effortlessly.

Navigation App

Imagine you’re inside a large MNC building. The place has multiple blocks and more than 5 floors. Finding the right meeting room, cafeteria, or washroom can be time-consuming, especially if you’re new. You are on the ground floor of Block A and need to go to a nearby meeting room.

  • Just enter your current location (“From”) and your destination (“To”).
  • The app instantly shows you the shortest path.
  • Since it’s on the same floor, you’ll only get turn-by-turn directions (e.g., move right, go straight, turn left).

No confusion, no wasted time. Totally, Whether it’s same floor, different floor, or even a different building, the app ensures you always find your way easily with clear step-by-step directions. This app works on offline.
